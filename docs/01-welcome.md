# 🤖 Overview
**Project Name**: SWAGI BloomBot<br />
**Purpose**: Stagnant Water Collector and Garden Irrigatorr

![robot 3d model](/img/robot.png)

This project aimed to solve stagnant water problem in SHS and JHS building grounds in AMNHS. It works by using a pump to collect water and a basic container to store water. Another pump is used to irrigate water through a hose, equipped with a servo motor to ensure even irrigation. Using 4 geared motors, it can move forward and backward, left and right, in differential steering. It uses WiFi communication to establish communication between an app in the operator's phone and the robot. It is powered by two 18650 Li-ion batteries (7.4V, 2200mAh), and can last for an hour or less on continuous used (based on testing).
